What We Do


We're organising a football tournament to raise funds who are in need of help. To participate, all you have to do is click on "Donate" button and it cost €2 for entry. We all hope to see you there and have fun!

At United Way, we welcome individuals, businesses, and organizations to join us in our mission. You can contribute through donations, volunteering, or becoming a partner in our various programs and initiatives. Together, we can create a brighter and more promising future for the children and adults we serve. Join us in making a difference today! Together, let's spread love, hope, and compassion to those in need.

At United Way, we are dedicated to making a positive impact in the lives of both kids and adults, fostering a brighter and more hopeful future for all. Our mission is to support and uplift vulnerable children and adults in need, providing them with the tools, resources, and opportunities they need to thrive.


To empower children and adults facing various challenges by offering them a helping hand, guidance, and support, nurturing their potential, and promoting their overall well-being.
